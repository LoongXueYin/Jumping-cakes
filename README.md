# 兔子躲蛋糕

一款移动端休闲小游戏。点击屏幕让兔子跳跃，躲避从两侧滑来的蛋糕，并把蛋糕一层层堆叠成山。

## 玩法

- **点击兔子**或**按空格键**跳跃
- 蛋糕从屏幕两侧交替滑来，跳过它就堆在脚下
- 踩到蛋糕侧面则游戏结束
- 堆得越高，画面越往上抬，视野越开阔

## 两种模式

| 模式 | 规则 |
|------|------|
| **街机模式** | 蛋糕悬空保留，轻松堆叠，适合休闲 |
| **拟真模式** | 每次只保留与上一层重叠的部分，切边掉落，更考验精准对齐 |

## 特性

- 移动端适配，触屏友好
- 6 首背景音乐，支持单曲循环或随机播放
- 设置界面可调节音乐/音效音量，选择背景音乐
- 暂停菜单支持继续、重开、设置、退出

## 运行

直接在浏览器中打开 `index.html` 即可。

> MP3 音乐文件需放置在项目根目录。

# Bunny vs Cake

A casual mobile web game. Tap to make the bunny jump — dodge cakes sliding in from both sides and stack them sky-high.

## How to Play

- **Tap the bunny** or **press Space** to jump
- Cakes slide in from alternating sides — jump over them to stack
- Getting hit on the side of a cake ends the game
- The higher you stack, the higher the camera rises

## Two Modes

| Mode | Rules |
|------|-------|
| **Arcade** | Cakes float in place, easy stacking — perfect for casual play |
| **Realistic** | Only the overlapping portion is kept; trimmed edges fall away — rewards precision |

## Features

- Mobile-first design with touch-friendly controls
- 6 background music tracks with single-loop or random shuffle
- Settings screen: adjust music/SFX volume, pick a BGM track
- Pause menu: resume, restart, settings, or quit

## Running

Open `index.html` in any browser.

> Place MP3 music files in the project root directory.
